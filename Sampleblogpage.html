What the B-2 is

    The B-2 spirit is a supersonic stealth bomber. it can carry up to 40,000 LBS of  bombs. It  is one of the most popular and powerful bombers in the world.
    
    
Why detecting the B-2 is nearly impossible

    The B-2 has never been detected on radar but sometimes when a B-2 approaches and opens it's bomb bay a small flicker sometimes appears on radar. 
    
    There are a few reasons why the B-2 almost never appears on radar. The reasons are as listed below:
    
    
   
  •  The engines are covered inside the wing making them less susceptible to enemy heat seeking missiles and radar
    • 
    
  • The lack of an afterburner ensures stealth from infrared homing
  • 
    
  • The composite material used in the B-2 bomber is specifically designed to absorb radio energy with optimum efficiency.
    •  
    
  • Parts of the B-2, such as the leading edge, are also covered in advanced radio-absorbent paint and tape. 
    •     
    
  • These materials are very expensive, and the Air Force has to reapply them regularly.
