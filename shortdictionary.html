SIMPLE DICTIONARY FOR NEW LEARNERS

  1. Hello
    2. A word that is commonly used as a greeting when meeting another person
  2. Diffusion
    3. The movement of solute particles from a region of high concentration to low concentration
  3. Osmosis
    4. The movement of solvent particles or water(in biology) from a region of high concentration to low concentration
  4. Latent Heat of Fusion
    5. The amount of heat energy required to change 1Kg of a solid to 1L of a liquid
